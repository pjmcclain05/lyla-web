Lyla Biosciences – for active people living with diabetes

Smarter Glucose Insights for Life with Diabetes

Heart rate × CGM insights in one app.

Lyla Biosciences is building a new layer of insight for glucose monitoring. By pairing CGM data with heart rate and activity information from Apple Health, Lyla helps people with Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes better understand how daily movement, workouts, stress, and routines may relate to changes in blood glucose. Our mission is to make glucose data more meaningful and transform isolated readings into clear patterns that support confidence and informed decision-making along with better insight for time in range, blood sugar trends, and health outcomes.
