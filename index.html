For Active people living with Diabetes

Smarter glucose insights for life with Diabetes.

Lyla is building a new layer of insight for continuous glucose monitoring. By pairing CGM data with heart rate and activity information from Apple Health, Lyla helps users better understand how daily movement, workouts, stress, and routines may relate to changes in blood glucose. Our mission is to make glucose data more meaningful and transform isolated readings into clear patterns that support confidence and informed decision-making. We believe better insight leads to better understanding, and better understanding leads to better outcomes.
