Why Lyla?

Traditional CGM apps show numbers and alarms. Lyla focuses on patterns, context, and what you can actually do about it.

People with T1D deserve better tools.

Hospital visits, scary lows and unexplained highs are often treated as random. But your data usually has a story. Lyla pulls together glucose, pulse, and activity to help you see what’s really going on.

We believe you’re not the problem.

Our experience suggests many people with Type 1 know what they “should” do, they just don’t have technology that makes it easy to understand how daily choices impact blood sugar. Lyla is designed to close that gap.

What is Lyla?

Lyla is a software layer built on top of your existing devices, designed to turn raw data into clear, human-readable insights.

More than a graph of your glucose.

Lyla blends CGM data with PPG signals (like pulse and movement) to understand when you’re eating, resting, moving, or under stress and how those states change your blood sugar.

From reactive to proactive.

Instead of only reacting to alarms, Lyla helps you spot consistent patterns: “I always rise after this breakfast,” or “I tend to drop overnight after this dose” and gives you suggestions you can test and refine over time.

What Lyla can help you see.

Whether you’re at school, at work, hanging out, or training, Lyla is built for everyday life with Type 1.

Continuous glucose monitoring

Connect to your CGM to see trends, not just single readings, with context about what else was happening at the time.

PPG and activity monitoring

Use pulse and movement data to understand how walking, workouts, sleep, and stress influence your blood sugar.

Integrated insights

See glucose, habits, and daily rhythms in one place so you can build strategies that are realistic for your life, not just for a perfect day.
